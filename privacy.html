SKYSTACK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

The short version

SKYSTACK does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and nothing is sent over the internet.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress — high scores, coins, unlocked skins, settings, missions, and daily streaks — is saved only in your browser's or device's local storage. It never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing site data deletes it permanently.

Permissions

The game uses vibration (haptics) if your device supports it and you leave it enabled in settings. No other device features are accessed.

Children

SKYSTACK is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children.

Future changes

If a future update adds features that involve data (for example, opt-in advertising), this policy will be updated first and the change noted in the app's store listing.
